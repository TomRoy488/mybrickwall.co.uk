Often referred to as brick tiles, brick slip tiles or brick veneers, brick slips are made from thin cuts of brick, purpose made for various applications. They are mainly used to mimic the look of a traditional brick wall and they are suitable for interior and exterior walls. Pre-cast brick slips are made from concrete or composite material and are moulded and designed to look and feel like real bricks.
